Ingredients

  • Chicken drumsticks or wings
  • Flour
  • Pepper
  • Eggs
  • Neutral Oil

Recipe

  1. Preheat the oil
  2. Dunk the chicken in an egg wash (beaten eggs)
  3. Dunk the egged chicken into flour and pepper mixture
  4. Repeat the previous two steps
  5. Fry until crispy but not black
