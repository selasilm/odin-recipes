Lasagna

A classic dish for those family nights in

Ah, lasagna. Lathan loves it, so you surely must as well. Cheesy, creamy, tomato-y and spaghettish, this is one you won't want to miss

Contrary to popular belief, this dish is enjoyed best fresh. Make just enough so that you're not eating nasty leftovers

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomatoes
  • 2 Onions
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Ground Beef
